Twitter Sentiment Analysis of popular IT course and course keyword.

Edspert product research was done by Sentiment analysis of 5 top popular IT courses and 8 course keywords. Edspert is a Widya Edu's product that focuses on bootcamps and online courses for students to workers.

Python Skillset:
  1. Basic: data cleaning and preparation (Numpy, Pandas, Matplotlib, scikit-learn).
  2. Intermediate: Exploratory Data Analysis/Visualization.
  3. Advanced: classification or clustering analysis, sentiment analysis.
Step:
  1. Data acquisition & pre-processing

    2. Tools: Python & Google Colaboratory
    Keywords of tweet are 5 top popular IT courses from Google Trends and Competitor Analysis. 5 top popular IT courses such as Python, Android, UI/UX, SQL, and AWS. Sentiment Analysis was also carried out to find the most popular course keywords among 'kursus', 'course', 'bootcamp', 'training', 'belajar', 'kelas', 'les', and 'pelatihan'.

    Scrape and clean tweet data by keyword from twitter in January - April 2022, import data to csv format.

  2. Sentiment Analysis

    3. Tools: Python & Google Colaboratory
    Classify tweet into neutral, positive, and negative tweet by library vader.

  3. Visualization

    4. Tools: Python & Google Colaboratory
    Visualize percentage of sentiments.

  4. Data storytelling

    5. Tools: Python & Google Colaboratory

    The most popular IT course from 5 Top Popular: UI/UX (4762 tweet: 77,15% neutral, 17,18% positive, 5,67% negative).
    3 Top popular keywords of UI/UX course: Belajar 63%, Bootcamp 16%, Course 9%.
